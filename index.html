Vendopro Privacy Policy

Effective Date:February 11, 2026

Vendopro (“we”, “us”, “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Vendopro collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use our mobile application (the “Vendopro App”).

1) Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the App:

  • Account Information: Email address for sign-in and account management. Passwords are handled securely by Firebase Authentication; we do not store your password directly.
  • Profile Information (Optional / User-Provided): Username, address (if you provide it), and avatar selection (avatarId).
  • Device / Machine Information: Machine ID(s) linked to your account and claim code used for machine verification.
  • Usage & Command Data: Commands you send to a machine (e.g., restart app/device) and timestamps (e.g., requestedAt).
  • App Settings: Selected machine stored locally on your device, language selection, and theme mode.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage your account
  • Authenticate users and keep accounts secure
  • Link your account to your machine(s)
  • Enable sending commands to your machine(s)
  • Display your profile and settings
  • Provide customer support and troubleshoot issues
  • Improve app reliability and user experience

3) Where Your Data Is Stored

We use Firebase (Google) services to store and process data, including Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore. Your data may be stored on Google/Firebase servers according to their terms and privacy practices.

4) Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only:

  • With service providers (Firebase/Google) necessary to operate the app
  • When required by law or to protect safety, security, and rights
  • As part of a business transfer (e.g., merger, acquisition)

5) Data Retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion where applicable.

6) Security

We use reasonable safeguards such as encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and access controls. No system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

7) Your Choices & Rights

You may update some information in the App. For requests like access or deletion, contact us below.

8) Children’s Privacy

Vendopro is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9) International Data Transfers

If you access the App from outside your region, your data may be processed internationally through Firebase infrastructure.

10) Third-Party Links

The App may open external links (e.g., Play Store). We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

11) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will revise the Effective Date when changes occur.
